Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

SellerFlow ("we", "our", or "us") operates a sales lead management platform that connects to your social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp) to help you manage customer conversations and orders.

Information We Collect

  • Account credentials (stored encrypted) for Instagram, Facebook Pages, and WhatsApp Business accounts you connect
  • Messages, comments, and conversations from your connected social media accounts
  • Lead and order data you create within the platform
  • Your email address and login credentials

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your social media conversations and comments in the SellerFlow dashboard
  • To automatically extract order/lead information from customer messages using AI
  • To send replies to customers on your behalf when auto-reply is enabled
  • To create and manage leads and orders in your account

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. Access tokens for social media platforms are encrypted at rest. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): We access your Page and Instagram data via the Meta Graph API under your authorization.
  • WhatsApp Business API: We use the WhatsApp Business API to send and receive messages on your behalf.
  • OpenRouter / Meta Llama: Customer messages are processed by an AI model to extract order information. Messages are sent to OpenRouter's API for this purpose.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect any social media account at any time from the SellerFlow dashboard. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.
